# Downloaded Papers

## Foundational
1. **Attention Is All You Need** (1706.03762_attention_is_all_you_need.pdf)
   - Authors: Vaswani et al., 2017
   - The original Transformer paper introducing self-attention as the sole mechanism

2. **BERT** (1810.04805_bert.pdf)
   - Authors: Devlin et al., 2019
   - Bidirectional transformer pre-training for NLP

## Surveys
3. **A General Survey on Attention Mechanisms in Deep Learning** (2203.14263_general_attention_survey.pdf)
   - Authors: Brauwers & Frasincar, 2022
   - Cross-domain survey with comprehensive taxonomy of attention types

4. **Attention Mechanisms in Computer Vision: A Survey** (2111.07624_attention_cv_survey.pdf)
   - Authors: Guo et al., 2022
   - Survey covering channel, spatial, temporal, and branch attention in CV

5. **A Comprehensive Survey on Applications of Transformers** (2306.07303_transformers_survey.pdf)
   - Authors: 2023
   - Broad survey of transformer applications across domains

6. **Automatic Speech Recognition using Advanced Deep Learning** (2403.01255_asr_deep_learning.pdf)
   - Authors: 2024
   - Survey of attention-based approaches in ASR

7. **Multimodal Alignment and Fusion** (2411.17040_multimodal_alignment.pdf)
   - Authors: 2024
   - Survey on cross-modal attention mechanisms

8. **The End of Transformers? Challenging Attention** (2510.05364_challenging_attention.pdf)
   - Authors: 2025
   - Critical examination of alternatives to attention (Mamba, RWKV, etc.)

9. **Comprehensive Review of Recommender Systems** (2407.13699_recommender_systems_review.pdf)
   - Authors: 2024
   - Review of attention-based recommendation systems

## Vision
10. **An Image is Worth 16x16 Words (ViT)** (2010.11929_vision_transformer.pdf)
    - Authors: Dosovitskiy et al., 2021
    - Pure transformer for image classification

11. **DALL-E: Zero-Shot Text-to-Image Generation** (2102.12092_dalle.pdf)
    - Authors: Ramesh et al., 2021
    - Transformer-based text-to-image generation

12. **Scalable Diffusion Models with Transformers (DiT)** (2212.09748_dit_diffusion_transformers.pdf)
    - Authors: Peebles & Xie, 2023
    - Transformer backbone for diffusion models (basis of Sora, SD3)

13. **Stable Diffusion 3** (2403.03206_stable_diffusion_3.pdf)
    - Authors: Esser et al., 2024
    - Multimodal Diffusion Transformer for image generation

## Audio/Music
14. **Whisper: Robust Speech Recognition** (2212.04356_whisper.pdf)
    - Authors: Radford et al., 2022
    - Transformer-based speech recognition

15. **Music Transformer** (1809.04281_music_transformer.pdf)
    - Authors: Huang et al., 2018
    - Attention-based music generation with relative attention

## Recommendation/Internet
16. **Self-Attentive Sequential Recommendation (SASRec)** (1808.09781_self_attentive_sequential_recommendation.pdf)
    - Authors: Kang & McAuley, 2018
    - Self-attention for recommendation systems

17. **Attention Calibration for Transformer-based Sequential Recommendation** (2308.09419_attention_calibration_recsys.pdf)
    - Authors: 2023
    - Improving attention in recommendation transformers

18. **From Features to Transformers: Redefining Ranking** (2502.03417_features_to_transformers_ranking.pdf)
    - Authors: LinkedIn, 2025
    - Production-scale transformer ranking system

## Graphs/Networks
19. **Graph Attention Networks** (1710.10903_graph_attention_networks.pdf)
    - Authors: Veličković et al., 2018
    - Attention on graph-structured data (social/citation networks)

## Protein/Biology
20. **AlphaFold-Multimer** (2112.11446_alphafold_multimer.pdf)
    - Authors: DeepMind, 2021
    - Attention mechanisms for protein structure prediction
